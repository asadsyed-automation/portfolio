Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical turnaround time?

For a standard blog post (1000-1500 words), I typically deliver within 3-5 business days. Longer articles (2000+ words) or bulk orders may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for urgent projects.

Do you offer unlimited revisions?

Yes. I want you to be completely satisfied. I'll revise the content as many times as needed to meet your expectations—no extra charges.

How do you conduct keyword research?

I use tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, and Google's own keyword planner to find relevant keywords with good search volume and manageable competition. I also analyze competitor content to identify gaps and opportunities.

What information do you need from me to start?

At minimum: your topic/keywords, target audience, desired word count, and any specific points to cover. Optionally: competitor URLs, brand voice guidelines, and reference materials.

Do you publish the content directly to WordPress?

I can deliver in any format you prefer—Google Docs, Microsoft Word, or direct WordPress upload (if you provide access). I can also format with headings, images, and meta tags.

Can you write for niches outside your listed expertise?

Possibly! I'm open to new niches as long as I can research them thoroughly. Contact me with your topic, and I'll let you know if it's a good fit.

What's your pricing?

Pricing varies based on word count, research depth, and project complexity. I offer competitive rates starting at fair per-word pricing. Contact me for a custom quote based on your needs.

Do you use AI tools to write content?

No. Every article is written by me from scratch. I may use AI for research assistance or grammar checks, but the writing, analysis, and strategy are 100% human.
